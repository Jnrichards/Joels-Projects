What are we?

Spread ur wings foundation is a non profit organisation we provide workshops and life skills for young people between the ages of 11-16, some of our activities include drama, singing, street dancing, rapping, photography, food and nutrition. Great Motivational speakers we also target more complex areas like the dangers of social media and child sex exploitation. Our teens enjoy our day trips out as well as our fun summer breaks away.
